# How to create your signature photo or scan

Your photo or scan will be stored securely and matched with your signature each time you vote by ((shortSourceType)).

Your vote might not be counted if the signatures do not look the same.

## 1. Sign your name

You’ll need black ink and plain white paper. Sign your name as you would normally.

Do not use:

* coloured inks
* coloured paper
* paper that’s creased
* paper with lines, squares or patterns

## 2. Take a photo or scan of your signature

## Steps for taking a photo

* You’ll need a smartphone or digital camera.
* Find a well-lit area so there are no shadows in the picture.
* Hold your phone or camera above the signature so all of it is captured.
* Make sure there is nothing showing around the edges, for example your desk.
* Save or transfer the photo to the device you’re using to apply.

## Steps for taking a scan

* You’ll need a scanner.
* Scan in your signature.
* Save the scan to the device you’re using to apply.

## 3. Check your photo or scan is OK and upload it

Your photo or scan must be:

* clear and in focus
* without shadows, lines, smudges or marks
* without anything showing around the edges, for example your desk
* the right way up
* all of your signature with nothing cut off
